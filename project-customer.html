About the Project

Understanding customer behavior is crucial for targeted marketing. This project applies **K-Means Clustering** to segment customers based on their purchasing habits, frequency, and monetary value (RFM Analysis).

The solution processes transactional data to identify distinct groups, allowing businesses to tailor their strategies for each segment, aiming to improve retention and maximize lifetime value.
